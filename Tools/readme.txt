Tool_findMaxandMin.py: Function to extract the min and max values of column 'vGRF' or 'TBF' from an Excel file, Used to normalize RMSE.
Tool_xlsx_to_txt.py:  In the COMSOL finite element analysis software, the input of tibial model parameters is a txt file. Now, the stored muscle force and ground reaction force in Excel are converted into a specified txt format.
Signal_preprocessing.py: Methods such as reading, storing, filtering, denoising, normalizing, and downsampling experimental data.
